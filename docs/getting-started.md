# Getting Started with CyberSoluce™

Welcome to CyberSoluce™, the strategic cybersecurity governance platform that orchestrates comprehensive security assessments with AI-powered intelligence across global frameworks.

## Quick Start Guide

### What is CyberSoluce™?

CyberSoluce™ is the **Governance Command Center** for modern cybersecurity operations. It provides:

- **Framework Fusion Technology™** - Intelligent crosswalk capabilities across multiple security frameworks
- **Intelligence Engine™** - AI-powered insights with predictive analytics
- **Executive Command Center** - Board-ready dashboards with strategic intelligence
- **Maturity Acceleration Engine** - Track and accelerate cybersecurity maturity progression

### Your First Assessment

Getting started with CyberSoluce is straightforward:

1. **Choose Your Domain**
   - Ransomware Defense (free assessment available)
   - Supply Chain Security
   - Privacy Protection
   - Sensitive Information Management

2. **Select Frameworks**
   - NIST CSF 2.0 (includes new Govern function)
   - ISO/IEC 27001:2022
   - CIS Controls v8
   - Regional frameworks (GDPR, CCPA, LGPD, PIPL)

3. **Configure Regional Settings**
   - North America (NIST focus)
   - European Union (GDPR compliance)
   - Asia Pacific (PIPL, PDPA)
   - Latin America (LGPD)

4. **Complete Assessment**
   - Answer maturity-based questions
   - Provide evidence where applicable
   - Review automated scoring

### Understanding Your Results

Your assessment results include:

- **Overall Maturity Score** (1-5 scale)
- **Framework Compliance Percentage**
- **Section-by-Section Breakdown**
- **Gap Analysis and Recommendations**
- **Executive Summary for Stakeholders**

## Core Concepts

### Security Domains

**Ransomware Defense**: Based on NIST IR 8374 and integrated with NIST CSF 2.0, focusing on the six core functions: Govern, Identify, Protect, Detect, Respond, and Recover.

**Supply Chain Security**: Aligned with NIST SP 800-161 Rev. 1, providing comprehensive supplier risk assessment and third-party governance capabilities.

**Privacy Protection**: Multi-jurisdictional compliance covering GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, LGPD, and PIPL with automated rights management and consent tracking.

**Sensitive Information**: CUI (Controlled Unclassified Information) protection based on NIST SP 800-171 with specialized controls for government contractors.

### Framework Fusion Technology™

Our proprietary technology automatically maps controls across different frameworks:

- **Intelligent Crosswalk**: Automatic control mapping with confidence scoring
- **Gap Analysis**: Identify overlaps and unique requirements
- **Unified Compliance**: Single dashboard for multiple framework compliance
- **Cross-Framework Reporting**: Generate reports spanning multiple standards

### Regional Adaptations

CyberSoluce adapts to regional requirements automatically:

- **Data Residency**: Comply with local data sovereignty laws
- **Regulatory Mapping**: Automatic adaptation to local regulations
- **Cultural Considerations**: Adapt assessment questions to local contexts
- **Regional Compliance**: Framework-specific adaptations for local regulations
